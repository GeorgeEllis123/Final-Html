Events

We host events every week and you can reserve dates to host your own personal events. To learn more visit our events page.

Menu

We have a vast pizza menu with other items. All ingredients are of the best quality and are all fresh. None of our ingredients are processed. We also have a custom pizza menu, with almost infinite different possible combinations. Visit our menu page for more information.

Delivery

We currently do not support delivery but hope to soon. Once delivery is made avaliable you will be able to see on our delivery page. Note that we will only be able to delivery within San Diego.





Chef


Giovanni Ferrari


Giovanni is a world renowned pizza chef with a dozen pizza restaurants around the world each with innumerable 5 star yelp reviews. Pepe's pizza is his most recent addition to his franchise. His countless years of practice making New York style pizza, is the reason he is know as the best pizza chef of this decade.
